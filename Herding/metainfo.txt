Name of Quantlet: Herding Detection in Cryptocurrency Markets

Description: "Empirical analysis of investor herding behavior in cryptocurrency markets using cross-sectional dispersion measures. The project applies CSSD and CSAD methodology to top cryptocurrencies, computes equal-weighted market log returns, and tests for herding under extreme market conditions through regression-based evidence. Visualizations include dispersion time series, market-return scatter plots, event annotations, and animated crypto-market behavior."
