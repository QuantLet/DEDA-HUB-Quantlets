Name of Quantlet: Predicting the Next Job Title from Job Advertisement Requirements

Description: "This Quantlet builds a text-classification pipeline to predict standardized O*NET occupation categories from job advertisement requirements text. The notebook loads real-world job advertisements from the OSF project pfhx3, joins the advertisements with O*NET job-title metadata, performs exploratory data analysis, transforms requirements_text into TF-IDF features, and compares a majority baseline, logistic regression, random forest, and multi-layer perceptron neural network. Model performance is evaluated using accuracy, weighted precision, weighted recall, macro F1, weighted F1, a confusion matrix, and class-level classification reports. The notebook also interprets predictive text features through logistic-regression coefficients and random-forest feature importances."
