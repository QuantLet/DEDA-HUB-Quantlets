Name of Quantlet: Multi-Party Computations for Machine Learning over Genomic Data

Description: "Implementation notebook for privacy-preserving genomic machine learning using Multi-Party Computation (MPC). The project builds a 3-party additive secret-sharing simulator from scratch and implements three classical genomic-ML primitives — allele frequency estimation, GWAS univariate regression, and Polygenic Risk Score (PRS) — in both vanilla NumPy and MPC variants. Uses 1000 Genomes chromosome 22 data (with synthetic fallback). Benchmarks MPC overhead in runtime, communication bytes, and round complexity vs. vanilla NumPy across a sweep of sample sizes and SNP counts, and visualizes the results."
